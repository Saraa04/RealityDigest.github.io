EDITOR'S NOTE

The website is built and managed by Sara Kitanoska, currently enrolled at the University of Bologna pursuing a Master’s Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge (DHDK).

The concept of the project is to organize articles in issues, as to convey the impression of a real magazine. Main preference of the magazine is to guide the reader/ viewer through these themes by creating a timeline of the carefully chosen articles from a collection of long reads on the web. The switchable themes are inspired by diverse periods over the centuries.

The Reality Digest is a monthly magazine, designed to attract different types of readers. In each issue you can find three articles covering a specific topic – all articles are shown at the same time as to allow a comparison between them. The main focus was to create documents as accurately as possible; hence, I put the priority on the historical accuracy, followed by the graphic design of the articles, and finally, on the responsiveness.

For those curious, techy readers who want to know more about the "behind-the-scenes" of this project - check out the full documentation on my GitHub repository.
